About

At Funny Cookie we believe that everyone deserves and needs a chuckle. We’re here to provide a quality chuckle moment. How many times have you fallen down a rabbit hole to some craptastic website that promised humor and delivered horrifying design and an 80’s user-experience? No more! Our mission is to help you find your chuckle, get your smile on and tickle your funny bone! Want more? Go here!

The idea for Funny Cookie came to me one day while I was in the midst of a software engineering bootcamp at Galvanize (thanks Galvanize!). Oh the stress! Oh the anxiety! I needed some laughter... Funny Cookie is now an award-winning website with millions of visitors daily!
